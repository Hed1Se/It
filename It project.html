It project: Memory Album

My ICT is about building a video or picture storage website.
The reason why I want to carry out this project is that my father often shows me pictures of me when I was a child.
But when I changed my mobile phone, the data was lost.


So I want to build a website to store videos or photos.
Users can store their videos or photos to avoid loss.
For the project, first of all, I will establish a database to store data.
Secondly, build a web page through which users can store their data into the database.
For the design of the website, I will collect users' opinions through online questionnaire,
so that users can easily use it after the website is running.
About my questionnaire, I chose the online questionnaire.
Through mobile communication, the main respondents are students and social workers in Ningbo.
The survey found that social workers are more interested in and willing to try our products.
